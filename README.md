             End-to-End Insurance Risk Analytics & Predictive Modeling

This project is mainly for AlphaCare Insurance Solutions (ACIS) a company that is committed to developing cutting-edge risk and predictive analytics in the area of car insurance planning and marketing in South Africa. 

                           objective
The aim is to help optimise the marketing strategy and discover “low-risk” targets for which the premium could be reduced, thereby creating an opportunity to attract new clients. 

Specifice Objectives:
   Analyze historical insurance claim data to identify patterns in claims, premiums, and loss ratios.

   Evaluate risk differences across provinces, postal codes, gender, and vehicle types.

   Support the design of data-driven strategies for optimal premium pricing.

   Build statistical and machine learning models to predict total claims and recommend adjustments in premiums based on vehicle, policyholder, and geographic features.

   Produce actionable insights that can enhance marketing campaigns and attract low-risk clients.
